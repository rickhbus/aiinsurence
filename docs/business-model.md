# Business Model

The MVP uses mock entitlements only. No real payment processing is active.

## Consumer Plans

- Free: Daily check-in, basic mood log, basic gym log, limited history, safety navigation.
- Plus HK$58/month: AI daily summary, food insights, mood coach, gym templates, weekly report.
- Pro HK$128/month: advanced trends, gym progression, doctor-visit summary export, insurance document checklist, deeper history.
- Family HK$198/month: 4-6 family members, consent caregiver sharing, family dashboard, elder/child placeholders, family weekly reports.

## B2B

- Gym/PT Partner: lead capture, member workout logs, adherence reports, trainer dashboard placeholder.
- Employer Wellness: privacy-safe aggregate wellness, challenges, stress education, no individual disclosure.
- Clinic/doctor preparation: visit-prep summaries, not diagnosis.

Business leads must not collect sensitive health, claim, policy, HKID, payment, or unnecessary personal data.
