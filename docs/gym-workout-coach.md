# Gym Workout Coach

The gym module records workout sessions, exercise sets, RPE, pain flags, energy, soreness, mood, notes, and AI summaries.

## Deterministic Safety First

Before any ordinary training guidance:

- Chest pain, fainting, severe dizziness, shortness of breath, severe sudden pain, or neurological symptoms stop the workout flow and escalate.
- Poor sleep plus high soreness suggests lighter training.
- High stress plus low energy suggests low-intensity movement.
- Pain on a specific movement suggests stopping that movement and considering professional guidance if persistent.

Emergency copy must direct Hong Kong users to call 999 or go to A&E now.

## Templates

Built-in templates cover Beginner Full Body 3 days/week, Push/Pull/Legs, Fat Loss, Muscle Gain, and Recovery/Mobility.

The module must not guarantee safety to exercise, treatment result, rehab outcome, or performance gains.
