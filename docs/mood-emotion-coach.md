# Mood & Emotion Coach

Mood capture includes score, stress, energy, emotion label, trigger category, body links, user text, reflection, small action, and safety flag.

Emotion Engine remains an assistive tone and clarity signal only. It is not a clinical assessment and must not affect insurance eligibility, pricing, coverage, care access, or claim outcomes.

## Wording

Use:

`你的訊息聽起來像是……`

Do not use:

`你患有……`

Self-harm, imminent danger, abuse, violence, or crisis language must trigger crisis/emergency guidance before ordinary mood coaching.
